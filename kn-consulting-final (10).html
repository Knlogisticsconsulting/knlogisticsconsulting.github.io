// About K&N Consulting

The training
behind the work.

K&N Consulting is led by Keilondre White, a MOS 0431 Logistics/Embarkation Specialist and United States Marine Corps veteran, trained at Camp Johnson, NC — one of the most demanding logistics roles in any branch of service.

As a 0431, Keilondre's mission was to ensure that cargo, personnel, and all other supplies arrived at the right place, at the right time, via the right mode of transport. Every operation was time-critical. Every decision carried real weight.

That experience builds a standard of execution that no corporate environment can replicate — and it's the same standard K&N Consulting brings to every business engagement.
